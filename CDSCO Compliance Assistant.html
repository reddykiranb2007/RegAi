Classification Guide

Class A - Low Risk

Risk Level: Minimal potential harm to user

Regulatory Authority: State Licensing Authority (SLA)

Detailed Requirements:

  • License: Registration Number (for non-sterile / non-measuring) or MD5 License (for sterile / measuring).
  • QMS: ISO 13485:2016 is voluntary but highly recommended for export and future-proofing.
  • Clinical Data: Generally exempt from Clinical Performance Evaluation, unless specified.
  • Device Master File (DMF): Basic technical file required.
  • Import: MD-14 Form required for authorized agents importing Class A devices.
  • Labeling: Must comply with CDSCO labeling standards (Rule 6).

Class B - Low Moderate Risk

Risk Level: Moderate potential harm; moderate controls needed

Regulatory Authority: State Licensing Authority (SLA)

Detailed Requirements:

  • License: MD5 (Manufacturing) or MD15 (Import) License required.
  • QMS: Mandatory compliance with ISO 13485:2016 (MDR 2017 Fifth Schedule).
  • Audit: Site audit by Notified Body (registered with CDSCO) required prior to grant of license.
  • Clinical Data: Clinical Investigation generally not required for predicate devices; Clinical Performance Evaluation may be needed.
  • Device Master File (DMF): Full Plant Master File (PMF) and Device Master File required.
  • Testing: Test license (MD-12) required for testing/analysis purposes if not yet licensed.

Class C - Moderate-High Risk

Risk Level: Significant potential harm; strict controls required

Regulatory Authority: Central Licensing Authority (CDSCO)

Detailed Requirements:

  • Authority: Central Drugs Standard Control Organization (CDSCO) - CLA.
  • License: MD9 (Manufacturing Loan) / MD5 or MD15 (Import).
  • QMS: Strict adherence to ISO 13485:2016 and MDR 2017 Fifth Schedule.
  • Audit: Mandatory inspection by Central Drug Inspector + State Drug Inspector.
  • Clinical Data: Clinical Investigation (Phase III) or robust Clinical Evaluation Report (CER) usually required.
  • Review: Subject to Subject Expert Committee (SEC) review for safety and efficacy.
  • Post-Market: Periodic Safety Update Reports (PSUR) may be required.

Class D - High Risk

Risk Level: Highest potential harm; strictest controls

Regulatory Authority: Central Licensing Authority (CDSCO)

Detailed Requirements:

  • Authority: Central Licensing Authority (CLA) - CDSCO HQ.
  • License: Stringent approval process for Manufacture (MD9) or Import (MD15).
  • QMS: Full Quality Assurance System per ISO 13485.
  • Clinical Data: Mandatory Clinical Investigation (Pilot + Pivotal) for new devices. Post-Marketing Clinical Follow-up (PMCF) often required.
  • Audit: Joint inspection by Central and State regulators with expert evaluation.
  • Safety: Strict Biocompatibility, Sterility, and Electrical Safety (IEC 60601) validation.
  • Post-Market: Mandatory Post-Market Surveillance (PMS) and vigilance reporting.
